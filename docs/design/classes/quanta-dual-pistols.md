## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** Entropic Echo · **Attunement × Archetype:** QUANTA × Dual Pistols
- **Primary stat:** SPD (← QUANTA) · **Secondary stat:** AGI (← Dual Pistols) — a SPD/AGI
  back-line probability-spray gunner *(from-brief)*
- **Resource:** QUANTA (party-shared; **fluctuates** — generates in bursts)
- **Attunement signature:** **probability swings** — crit / dodge / time, **no DoT**. QUANTA's
  "DoT-that-isn't" is **Doom** (a delayed, *determined* hit — nothing ticks). Avoidance is a single
  axis, **dodge %** (canon: no separate untargetable/immune state; "can't be hit" = dodge toward
  ~100%). The accuracy/crit debuff is **Decohere**. The signature beat is **Superposition →
  Collapse** — here built by *fire* and reused *across the line*, not on one foe.

**Fantasy.** *(from-brief)* The Entropic Echo is the **probability-spray gunner** — the Gunslinger
who fires a round and lets it **echo**. A single shot does not resolve once; it **ricochets** into a
spread of possible impacts that **reverberate** across the whole enemy line, each its own crit/dodge
swing. Where the Rifle's Observer Prime *observes one target and collapses one fate* into a single
precise shot, the Entropic Echo holds the **whole line in superposition** under a hail of caroming
fire and **collapses the volley** — and where the melee Anomaly *steals turns*, the Echo never
touches the clock: it kites from the back row and answers fragility with **dodge%**, burying the
line under reverberating rounds. It frays the enemy's coherence with **Decohere** so their blades
fail, and stamps **Doom at range** so a foe's hour is fixed while the volley keeps ringing.
**Volume, not the one shot; outcome-spray, not turn-theft.**

### The shared Gunslinger DNA *(from-brief — how this is a Dual Pistols)*

1. **Double-tap.** Dual barrels fire two rounds — two crit rolls / two echo-seeds per trigger-pull;
   the auto and many specials resolve as a paired report.
2. **Volume / rhythm.** Sustained fire builds the **echo** — cheap fast shots accumulate observation
   (Superposition) across the line, feeding the finisher. The rhythm of fire *is* the resource curve.
3. **Back-line kite.** Fragility is answered by staying at range and **dodge%** — reposition,
   quickdraw, slip the return fire; never armor, never lockdown.
4. **Opening → Finisher reuses QUANTA's own phase chain, applied by fire.** No new combo resource:
   the Opening **is** accumulated **Superposition**, sprayed onto the line by every echoing round;
   the Finisher **Collapses** that superposition into a determined volley.

**Build axes:** volume-burst-now ↔ enemy-offense-denial (A↔B) · coherence-denial ↔ Doom-attrition
(B↔C) · own-line damage ↔ enemy-line denial (A ↔ C) · certainty-on-my-volley ↔
certainty-on-their-doom (A ↔ C).

**Cross-lane synergy:** **B Decoheres the line so its blows fray and your swings land → C stamps
Doom so each foe's hour is fixed while you keep firing → A sprays the line into Superposition and
collapses the echo into a guaranteed multi-hit volley across every Decohered, Doomed foe at once.**

### Distinctness *(invariants #9 & #10 — how this seat is honored)*

- **Same-archetype (#9) — vs the other four Dual Pistols (the Gunslinger family).** Entropic Echo is
  the **probability-spray gunner**: ricochet/echo rounds, crit/dodge swings, Decohere, Doom-at-range.
  Distinct from SOL **Gunslinger Solaris** (radiant fan-fire crit-spray that rakes Burn — pure
  offense, no probability), NOX **Cryovex** (Chill-volley → Frozen → shatter-shot), ANIMA
  **Sporecaster** (Infestation-spore spray, non-healer), and UMBRAXIS **Orbitalist** (gravity-curved
  rounds that pull/cluster + Drain). His rounds *echo and collapse* — outcome-control fire, owned by
  no other Gunslinger.
- **Same-archetype — vs his Rifle cousin Observer Prime (QUANTA Rifle, "the one shot").** They share
  only the QUANTA **signature** (probability swings, Doom, Decohere, Superposition → Collapse).
  Observer Prime is the **single-target precision observer**: observe *one* target, collapse *one*
  fate into a single decisive shot, first-strike. Entropic Echo is the **volume opposite**: it never
  observes one fate — it sprays the *whole line* into superposition and **collapses the volley** into
  many ricocheting hits. One collapse-shot vs the ricochet-volley; precision vs spray.
- **Same-archetype — vs the melee QUANTA Anomaly (daggers, "turn-economy assassin").** The Anomaly
  *steals turns* — it manipulates the ATB clock to act more than anything. Entropic Echo touches
  **no turn-economy at all**: it is **ranged probability-spray**, kiting from the back row, not
  stealing the enemy's actions. Its lever is **echo/ricochet volume + Decohere + ranged Doom**, never
  turn-theft.
- **Same-attunement (#10) — QUANTA concept budget.** He reuses the QUANTA *signature* freely
  (probability swings, Doom, Decohere, Superposition → Collapse) as the framework intends, but takes
  the one role no other QUANTA class holds: **ranged echo/ricochet volume — a single round
  resolving into many across the line, collapsed.** He does **not** pile onto a saturated QUANTA
  role — the **single-target crit/Doom executioner** is the Hammer (Causality Arbiter), the
  **turn-economy assassin** the daggers (The Anomaly), the **evasive phase-duelist** the swords
  (Phasewalker), the **AoE momentum line-cleaver** the Two-Handed Sword (Timeline Breaker), the
  **support/enchanter** the Staff (Chronosage), the **evasion-tank** the Sword & Shield (Paradox
  Bastion), and the **precision observer** the Rifle (Observer Prime). The ranged ricochet-volley
  seat is held by no other QUANTA class. (QUANTA "no DoT" honored: Doom is a delayed *determined*
  hit, nothing ticks; avoidance is dodge %, no immune state.)
