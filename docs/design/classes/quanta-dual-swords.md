## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** Phasewalker · **Attunement × Archetype:** QUANTA × Dual Swords
- **Primary stat:** SPD (← QUANTA) · **Secondary stat:** AGI (← Dual Swords) — a SPD/AGI
  evasion-duelist *(from-brief)*
- **Resource:** QUANTA (generates & spends the party's shared QUANTA pool; **fluctuates** —
  generates in bursts)
- **Attunement signature:** **probability swings** — crit / dodge / time, **no DoT**. QUANTA's
  "DoT-that-isn't" is **Doom** (a delayed, *determined* hit — nothing ticks). Avoidance is a single
  axis, **dodge %** (canon: no separate untargetable/immune state; "can't be hit" = dodge toward
  ~100%). The accuracy/crit debuff is **Decohere**.

**Fantasy.** An evasive **phase-duelist** who wins the exchange by controlling **space**, not time.
Where the QUANTA dagger (The Anomaly) steals turns, the Phasewalker steps *between* the blades:
blinks in to land a measured crit and blinks out before the answer comes, leaves an afterimage so
the enemy strikes where they no longer are, and — when caught in the exchange — parries by **phasing
through the blow itself**, then answers with a counter whose outcome is already written. Survives by
dodging toward 100%; wins on crit, evasion, and the riposte. A stand-and-duel blade that simply
*isn't there* when it loses. *(from-brief: seat = evasive phase-duelist; fragility = dodge%; controls
space, not time; parry-riposte, not turn-steal.)*

### Lanes *(from-brief — locked)*

| Lane | Identity | Keys off | Team role | Best when |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A · Phase Edge** | blink-strike crit: phase in, land a measured crit, phase out — the spatial duel, raising crit amplitude and collapsing it to a clean critical exchange | **AGI**, Crit chance/damage substats | single-target carry / spike | gear-rich crit build vs squishy targets & elites |
| **B · Afterimage** | dodge/evasion via phase: leave afterimages so attacks resolve to misses (dodge → ~100% window) and **Decohere** the enemy so its blades fray | **SPD/AGI**, evasion / dodge substats | front-row survivor / disruptor | holding the line solo; vs hard-hitting single attackers |
| **C · Riposte Paradox** | parry by phasing *through* the blow, then a determined counter: convert avoided hits into ripostes and stamp **Doom** so the counter's outcome is fixed | **AGI**, counter / Doom, dodge | counter-duelist / outcome-controller | drawn-out duels; tanky foes; reactive comps |

**Build axes:** crit-burst ↔ dodge-survival (A↔B) · being-elsewhere ↔ answering-back (B↔C) ·
my-crit-certain ↔ their-miss-certain (A↔B) · proactive strike ↔ reactive counter (A,B ↔ C).

**Shared duelist DNA woven in** *(from-brief)*: **AGI crit** on the auto and the Phase Edge line; the
**parry-riposte** — here a parry *by phasing through* the blow (Riposte Paradox), the dual-sword's
survival tool; **flow/stance** as held phase-states that build the exchange; and the **Opening →
Finisher** beat reusing QUANTA's own phase-transition chain **Superposition → Collapse** (no new
combo resource — accumulated *observation* from strikes is the Opening, and a finisher collapses it).
Fragility is answered by **dodge%**, never armor.
