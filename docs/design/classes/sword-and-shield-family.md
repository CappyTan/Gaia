# Sword & Shield — the class family (5 stances on harm) — RATIFIED CANON

> **Status: RATIFIED CANON (Dara, 2026-06-28).** The family design for the five Sword & Shield
> classes (one per Attunement). All five specs are written and blessed; this note is the shared frame
> they honor — five deliberate variations, not five copies. Draws on the ratified
> [Attunement Mechanics Framework](../attunement-mechanics.md).

## The organizing principle

The Sword & Shield archetype answers one question — **"what do you do about incoming harm?"** — and
**each Attunement answers it differently.** This parallels the framework's "five stances on entropy":
the **five S&S classes are five stances on harm.** All five share **DEF as secondary** (so all are
*durable*), but each one's **Attunement primary** pushes it into a distinct role and a **distinct
defensive mechanic** — no two overlap.

| Attunement × S&S | Canon name | Primary + DEF | Defensive mechanic | Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **UMBRAXIS** | Tidal Sovereign | **DEF + DEF** | **Damage reduction** — raw mass/absorb; gravity pulls aggro; drain to sustain | pure **wall** tank |
| **NOX** | Penumbral Bastion | STR + DEF | **Mitigate-magnitude** — freeze/slow the blow + stillness armor; punishes back | **control-tank** |
| **QUANTA** | Paradox Bastion | SPD + DEF | **Dodge %** — collapse the attack to a miss *(no immune state — see avoidance rule)* | **evasion-tank** |
| **SOL** | Dawnwarden | AGI + DEF | **Block / block-stacking** — active, gear-dependent | **damage off-tank** |
| **ANIMA** | Soul-Bound Aegis | VIT + DEF | **Summoned life + barriers + entangle + heal** | **summoner-protector** |

Three are dedicated tanks, each a different *physics of not-dying* (**absorb / mitigate / evade**);
two break the mold because their Attunement isn't defensive — **SOL** (pure offense → a damage
off-tank) and **ANIMA** (life → a protector). This gives players a real menu: pick a tank *flavor*,
or a durable front-liner that *also* deals damage / sustains the party.

## Per-class sketches

### QUANTA · Paradox Bastion — **built** ✓
Evasion-tank; avoidance via dodge %. Full spec: [`quanta-sword-and-shield.md`](./quanta-sword-and-shield.md).
Lanes: Blurguard (self-dodge) · Wardshift (interpose) · Decoherence (enemy-offense denial + Doom).

### SOL · Dawnwarden — damage off-tank (block-stacking)
Default identity is **damage**; main-tanking is a *gear investment*. Mitigation is **Block**, and the
hook is **stacking Block** — build stacks (each adds block chance/amount) so a block-geared Dawnwarden
stacks high enough to **main-tank**, while ungeared it's a bruiser that off-tanks. SOL turns defense
outward, so **blocking should feed offense** (block → counter / radiant burst, or generates SOL).
- **A · Aggressor** — the damage engine: shield-bash, shield-throw, radiant strikes, Burn/Blind, AoE slams (spread). *(AGI / offense)*
- **B · Bastion Stacks** — the block-stacking tank path; stack Block to become main-tank-viable. *(DEF / block gear)*
- **C · Riposte** — converts defense into offense: a successful block triggers a counter / radiant retaliation / thorns. The bridge — rewards blocking *and* hits back. *(block → damage)*

*Gear tips the optimum: fresh = A+C block-and-punish bruiser; block-geared = drop into B to main-tank.*

### ANIMA · Soul-Bound Aegis — summoner-protector (three kingdoms of life)
A VIT+DEF protector who defends the party with **summoned life**. The three lanes are three **kinds of
life that behave differently**, and each summon can **Evolve** mid-fight (ANIMA's signature —
Seed→Bloom→Overgrowth changes a summon's form).
- **A · Guardian** *(fauna)* — one big, durable **beast body-blocker**: taunts, soaks hits for the party. *Protection by soaking. (few, tough)*
- **B · Thornwild** *(flora)* — spreading **plants that entangle/root/snare** enemies (rooted/disarmed → their attacks never land) and raise thorn-walls. *Protection by debuffing the enemy's offense — the living mirror of QUANTA's Decoherence. (control/deny)*
- **C · Symbiote** *(symbiosis)* — **regenerative life grafted onto allies**: heal, regen, link vitality. *Protection by sustain. (buff/heal organism)*

### NOX · Penumbral Bastion — control-tank *(sketch; detail at build time)*
Mitigates by **lowering the magnitude** — freeze/slow incoming attacks, stillness/lattice armor — and
**punishes back** (STR). Takes *little* when hit. Likely lanes echo a control/mitigate/punish split;
reconcile with the NOX suite + the Equilibrium Ascendant's shared NOX vocabulary at build time.

### UMBRAXIS · Tidal Sovereign — pure wall tank *(sketch; detail at build time)*
The DEF+DEF immovable wall: **raw damage reduction + absorb**, **gravity** to pull aggro and lock foes
in place, **drain** to self-sustain. The purest tank of the five; detail with the (pending) UMBRAXIS suite.

## Ratified decisions & notes
- **SOL lane C = Riposte** (block→counter), ratified over the Radiance/AoE-control alternative.
- **Summon persistence (ANIMA): per-battle** — summoned creatures are conjured within a fight and reset between battles; no out-of-combat menagerie to manage.
- **"Block" vs "Dodge" are distinct defensive mechanics** (ratified): SOL *reduces/negates a portion & stacks*; QUANTA *avoids entirely* (dodge %). Both belong in the engine's defensive model.
- **Entangle/Root** (ANIMA Thornwild) shares a mechanic shape with UMBRAXIS's gravity **Anchored** — same effect, different flavor (vines vs gravity); a single underlying "rooted" status can serve both.
