## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** The Anomaly · **Attunement × Archetype:** QUANTA × Dual Daggers
- **Primary stat:** SPD (← QUANTA) · **Secondary stat:** SPD (← Dual Daggers) — **SPD+SPD, the
  fastest possible class** *(from-brief)*
- **Resource:** QUANTA (generates & spends the party's shared QUANTA pool)
- **Attunement signature:** **probability swings** — crit / dodge / time, **no DoT**. QUANTA's
  "DoT-that-isn't" is **Doom** (a delayed, *determined* hit — nothing ticks). Avoidance is a single
  axis, **dodge %** (canon: no separate untargetable/immune state; "can't be hit" = dodge toward
  ~100%). The accuracy/crit debuff is **Decohere**.

**Fantasy.** A pure crit-and-tempo **probabilist** — the fastest thing alive — who treats a fight as
a wavefunction to be measured. It does not strike *and hope*; it raises the **amplitude** of the
outcome it wants, takes its **twin measurements**, and **collapses** the result to certainty. It
out-races the ATB bar to act more often than anyone, manipulates turn-order outright, and survives on
the front row by **dodging toward 100%** rather than soaking a single hit. The flagship of the dagger
family — the template-setter. *(from-brief: seat, fragility = dodge%, twin-strike applicator)*

### Lanes *(from-brief — locked)*

| Lane | Identity | Keys off | Team role | Best when |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A · Amplitude** | crit-amplification burst: raise the probability amplitude of a critical outcome (stack crit), twin-strike to take two measurements, then **collapse the wavefunction to a guaranteed maximal crit** | **SPD**, Crit chance/damage substats | single-target nuke / spike carry | gear-rich crit build vs squishy targets & elites |
| **B · Probability Storm** | the tempo engine: chance of extra turns, Haste, **rewind** a missed strike, **pre-empt** the enemy (foresight) — acts more than anything in the game | **SPD**, action-economy / turn-priority | tempo enabler / first-mover | racing the boss's cast; SPD-stacked party that wants the turns |
| **C · Collapse** | dodge-survival + Doom: collapse incoming attacks to misses (dodge→~100%), **Decohere** the enemy, stamp **Doom** and resolve it | **SPD**, evasion / dodge substats | front-row survivor / outcome-controller | holding the line solo; vs hard-hitting single attackers |

**Shared rogue DNA woven in** *(from-brief)*: **twin-strike** (double roll) on the auto and many
specials; an **Opening → Finisher** beat where finishers spend QUANTA's own phase-transition chain
**Superposition → Collapse** (no new combo-point resource — the Opening *is* accumulated observation);
a **tempo lean** on every lane (SPD is doubled); and **fragility answered by dodge%**, never armor.

**Build axes:** burst ↔ tempo (A↔B) · self-carry ↔ team turn-economy (A,B ↔ B) · offense ↔
dodge-survival (A,B ↔ C) · certainty-on-my-hit ↔ certainty-on-their-miss (A ↔ C).
