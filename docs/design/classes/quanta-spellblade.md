## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** Quantum Exarch · **Attunement × Archetype:** QUANTA × Spellblade
- **Primary stat:** SPD (← QUANTA) · **Secondary stat:** AGI (← Spellblade) — a SPD/AGI front-line
  probability battle-mage *(from-brief)*
- **Resource:** QUANTA (party-shared; **fluctuates** — generates in bursts, spent on the collapse)
- **Attunement signature:** **probability swings** — crit / dodge / time, **no DoT**. QUANTA's
  "DoT-that-isn't" is **Doom** (a delayed, *determined* hit — nothing ticks). Avoidance is a single
  axis, **dodge %** (canon: no separate untargetable/immune state; "can't be hit" = dodge toward
  ~100%). The accuracy/crit debuff is **Decohere**. The signature beat is **Superposition → Collapse**
  — reused here through the **imbued blade**: the strike *observes* (loads Superposition), the spell
  *collapses* it to a determined hit.

**Fantasy.** *(from-brief)* The Quantum Exarch is the **probability battle-mage** — the Runeblade who
*observes with the strike and collapses with the spell*, at the front line. He does not stand at the
back and enchant the fight from a distance; he carries the wavefunction into melee range. Each imbued
cut is a **measurement**: it crits, it **Decoheres** the foe, and it loads a sliver of **Superposition**
onto the target — the future left undetermined. Then the spell **collapses** that loaded uncertainty
into a single determined outcome: a guaranteed crit, a sealed **Doom**, a foe whose next blow resolves
to a miss. Caught in the exchange, he holds himself in superposition and the enemy's blade finds an
Exarch who *isn't quite there*. Where the QUANTA Staff (Chronosage) is the **back-line time/probability
enchanter** who reorders the whole battle from safety, the Exarch **fights at the front** and **fuses**
strike-observation with spell-collapse on the target in front of him — not a support loom, a melee
caster whose blade and spell are one measurement. Strike to load, cast to collapse; the outcome was
written the instant he looked.

### The shared Runeblade DNA *(from-brief — how this is a Spellblade)*

1. **Imbue** — channel QUANTA into the blade; strikes carry the signature (crit / **Decohere** /
   loaded **Superposition** applied in melee), and the charge can be released as a spell-collapse.
2. **Strike↔Cast rhythm** — melee strikes **generate** QUANTA (specials); spells **spend** it
   (signatures). The Exarch is the most literal expression of the MNA generate→spend economy: the
   strike *observes* and banks observation, the cast *collapses* and spends it.
3. **Front-line caster** — he casts at melee range and survives there, *unlike the back-line Staff*.
   Fragility is answered by **dodge%** (hold himself in superposition), never armor, never lockdown.
4. **Reuse QUANTA's own phase chain via the imbued blade** — no new combo resource: the Opening **is**
   accumulated **Superposition** (loaded by imbued strikes), and the finisher is a spell that
   **Collapses** it to a determined hit on the target.

**Build axes:** blade-melee ↔ spell-reach (A↔B) · spell-reach ↔ fusion-loop (B↔C) · raw-strike
crit ↔ collapsed determined-outcome (A ↔ C) · proactive observe ↔ released collapse (A,B ↔ C).

**Cross-lane synergy:** **A's imbued strikes load Superposition and Decohere the target → C collapses
that loaded uncertainty into a determined hit through the blade → B reaches the rest of the line with
Doom and dodge-swings while the Exarch holds himself out of phase.**

## Distinctness *(invariants #9 & #10 — how this seat is honored)*

- **Same-archetype (#9) — vs the four other Spellblades (the Runeblade family).** The Quantum Exarch is
  the **probability battle-mage**: observe with the strike, collapse with the spell. Distinct from the
  SOL **Starforge Knight** (radiant battle-mage — strike to stoke Burn, cast to Detonate; pure
  offense), the NOX **Lattice Executioner** (freeze by spell, Shatter by blade — frost executioner),
  the ANIMA **Biomancer** (blade-borne contagion + self-mutation, non-healer), and the UMBRAXIS
  **Voidstar Exarch** (drain-blade + gravity-collapse, durable DEF tank). His fusion is uniquely
  **observe→collapse**: the strike *measures* (loads Superposition + Decohere), the spell *resolves it
  to a determined outcome* — a mechanic no sibling shares.
- **Same-archetype — vs his back-line cousin Chronosage (QUANTA × Staff).** The Chronosage is the
  **back-line time/probability enchanter** — a support/loom that hastens allies, stalls foes, and bends
  party-wide crit/dodge from safety. The Quantum Exarch **fights at the FRONT**, fuses strike-observe
  with spell-collapse on the *target in front of him*, and answers fragility with **dodge%** rather than
  hiding. Enchanter-of-the-party vs melee-caster-of-the-target; back row vs front row.
- **Same-attunement (#10) — QUANTA concept budget.** He reuses the QUANTA *signature* freely — the
  ratified suite (Superposition → Collapse, Doom, Decohere, dodge%, no DoT) — as the framework intends.
  He does **not** pile onto a saturated QUANTA *role*: the **back-line enchanter/support** is the Staff
  (Chronosage), the **turn-economy assassin** is the dagger (The Anomaly), the **phase-dodge duelist**
  is the dual swords (Phasewalker), the **single-target determined-verdict crusher** is the Hammer
  (Causality Arbiter), the **AoE momentum line-cleaver** is the greatsword (Timeline Breaker), the
  **ranged precision sniper** is the Rifle (Observer Prime), the **ricochet-volley line-spray** is the
  Dual Pistols (Entropic Echo), and the **evasion speed-tank** is the Sword & Shield (Paradox Bastion).
  The Exarch's seat — *the melee-caster strike-observe → spell-collapse fusion at the front line* — is
  held by no other QUANTA class.
