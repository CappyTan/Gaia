## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** Observer Prime · **Attunement × Archetype:** QUANTA × Rifle
- **Primary stat:** SPD (← QUANTA) · **Secondary stat:** SPD (← Rifle) — the **SPD+SPD doubled-stat
  flagship** of the back row *(from-brief)*
- **Resource:** QUANTA (party-shared; **fluctuates** — generates in bursts)
- **Attunement signature:** **probability swings** — crit / dodge / time, **no DoT**. QUANTA's
  "DoT-that-isn't" is **Doom** (a delayed, *determined* hit — nothing ticks). Avoidance is a single
  axis, **dodge %** (canon: no separate untargetable/immune state; "can't be hit" = dodge toward
  ~100%). The accuracy/crit debuff is **Decohere**. The signature beat is **Superposition →
  Collapse** — reused here **as the decisive single shot**.

**Fantasy.** *(from-brief)* Observer Prime is the **precision observer** — the marksman who does not
shoot and hope, but *measures* and *resolves*. He holds the target in the long stillness of the scope,
watches its wavefunction spread across every outcome it might take, and at the chosen instant
**collapses that wavefunction down the barrel** — a single round whose result was already determined
before it left the muzzle: a guaranteed, maximal hit. He **marks** a target so its fate is written,
**Decoheres** the line so its returning fire frays, and — being the fastest eye on the field
(SPD+SPD) — sees the enemy's move *before* it commits and **pre-empts** it, opening from max range and
acting before the line. He is not the dagger that *steals* the enemy's turns (The Anomaly) and not the
pistols that *bury* the line in fire (Entropic Echo): he removes **one observed target** with **one
collapsed shot**. The long observation, then the foregone round.

### The shared Marksman DNA *(from-brief — how this is a Rifle)*

1. **The aimed shot** — single-target precision burst, *the one shot*, never volume. Observer Prime's
   aimed shot is a **collapsed certainty**: the wavefunction resolved to a determined, guaranteed hit,
   not a hopeful one.
2. **Charge / aim (the long observation)** — a wind-up that loads a bigger shot. Here the wind-up *is*
   **observation**: the longer he holds the target in the scope, the more of its superposition he has
   measured, and the larger the collapse when he fires (the ranged cousin of the Greatsword's
   momentum — but measured, not muscled).
3. **The mark** — designate a target so its outcome is fixed: stamp **Doom** + **Decohere**, then
   resolve the determined shot from range. The mark is the marksman's contract; here it is a *worldline
   already sealed*.
4. **First-strike / range control** — SPD priority to act first. Observer Prime opens the fight from
   max range and, doubled on SPD, sees and **pre-empts** the enemy's move (foresight), keeping the
   initiative. His fragility answer is **strike first and stay out of reach** (range + first-strike),
   never armor or mass.
5. **Opening → Finisher reuses QUANTA's own phase chain as the decisive shot.** No new combo resource:
   the Opening **is** accumulated **Superposition** on the marked target (built by autos, the long
   observation, and reach-stamps); the Finisher **Collapses** it into one determined, guaranteed round.

### Distinctness *(invariants #9 & #10 — how this seat is honored)*

- **Same-archetype (#9) — vs the four other Rifles (the Marksman family).** Observer Prime is the
  **precision observer**: observe → collapse → one determined shot, with mark and first-strike control.
  Distinct from the SOL **Photon Vanguard** (a charged radiant *beam* that pierces the line, Burn/Blind
  at range), the NOX **Terminus** (the freezing *kill-shot* — mark → Frozen → Shatter-shot, a low-HP
  execute), the ANIMA **Genewarden** (precision *gene-injection* — mark → Infestation/mutation,
  non-healer), and the UMBRAXIS **Astrolancer** (a piercing *gravity-lance* that Anchors/Drains). His
  round is *determined* — outcome-control and observation, not beam, freeze, contagion, or gravity; and
  he is the **doubled-SPD flagship** of the family.
- **Same-archetype — vs his Pistols cross-cousin Entropic Echo (QUANTA Dual Pistols, "volume").** They
  share only the QUANTA **signature** (probability swings, Doom, Decohere, Superposition → Collapse).
  Entropic Echo is the ranged **volume-fire** gunner: ricocheting *echo* shots where one round resolves
  into several across the whole line — spray and crit-swing the field. Observer Prime is the **single
  collapse-SHOT**: one observed target, one determined maximal round, mark + first-strike. The Echo
  sprays the line; the Observer collapses *one* fate. No ricochet/echo/volley in this kit at all.
- **Same-archetype — vs the melee SPD+SPD twin, The Anomaly (QUANTA Dual Daggers).** This is the
  critical distinction (both are doubled-SPD). The Anomaly is the **melee turn-economy assassin** — it
  *steals turns*: chance of extra turns, time-skip a foe, rewind, act more often than anything alive, on
  the front row by dodge%. Observer Prime is the **ranged precision observer** — it does **not** steal
  turns. Its SPD is spent on **first-strike / foresight / pre-empt** (act *before* the line, once) and
  on the **long observation** that loads the collapse-shot — *range control and a single decisive
  round*, not a turn-economy engine. Different row (back vs front), different lever (one foreseen
  collapse-shot vs stolen turns). It has **no** extra-turn/turn-steal/time-skip/rewind tooling.
- **Same-attunement (#10) — QUANTA concept budget.** He reuses the QUANTA *signature* freely
  (probability swings, Doom, Decohere, Superposition → Collapse, dodge%) as the framework intends, but
  takes the one role no other QUANTA class holds: the **ranged single-target collapse-SHOT — observe →
  collapse → determined round, with mark + first-strike range control.** He does **not** pile onto a
  saturated QUANTA role — the **single-target crit/Doom *melee* executioner** is the Hammer (Causality
  Arbiter), the **AoE line-collapse cleaver** the Two-Handed Sword (Timeline Breaker), the
  **turn-economy assassin** the daggers (The Anomaly), the **evasive phase-duelist** the swords
  (Phasewalker), the **evasion-tank** the Sword & Shield (Paradox Bastion), and the **support/enchanter**
  the Staff (Chronosage). The ranged observer-marksman seat is held by no other QUANTA class. (QUANTA
  "no DoT" honored: Doom is a delayed *determined* hit, nothing ticks; avoidance is dodge %, no immune
  state.)
