# Null Absolutionist — NOX × Staff

> **Status:** greenfield spec (Phase 2 kit). Lanes from the [Staff family note](./staff-family.md);
> every ability `proposed`. Numberless. Mechanics from the ratified
> [Attunement Mechanics Framework](../attunement-mechanics.md). Energy operation: **remove**. Leans
> **null / silence / void** to stay distinct from the three other NOX classes (Rimewalker, Equilibrium
> Ascendant, Penumbral Bastion) — energy-removal, not frost-attack or shadow.

## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** Null Absolutionist · **Attunement × Archetype:** NOX × Staff
- **Primary stat:** STR (← NOX) · **Secondary stat:** VIT (← Staff)
- **Resource:** NOX (party-shared; **banks**) · **Signature:** Stasis / Freeze / Seal
- **Energy op:** **Remove** — take energy *out of the system*. Caster role: **control / anti-caster**.

**Fantasy.** The Null Absolutionist takes energy *out of the system*. Where the Heliomancer pours heat
in, the Absolutionist drinks it back toward absolute zero — silencing spells, freezing motion,
dispelling power, draining the field to stillness and dark. The anti-mage: it doesn't out-damage you,
it makes you *unable to act*. Entropy reversed by subtraction.

### Lanes *(three flavors of negation)*

| Lane | Identity | Best when |
|---|---|---|
| **A · Silence** | anti-magic — Seal abilities, lock cooldowns, forbid buffs/casts | vs caster-heavy enemies |
| **B · Heat Sink** | energy-drain — sap MP/resource & tempo, Chill toward stillness | vs fast / resource-reliant foes |
| **C · Unmaking** | erasure — strip buffs, cleanse-lock, unmake summons/wards & fields | vs buffed / summoner enemies |

**Build axes:** disable-actions ↔ drain-energy (A↔B) · disable ↔ erase-effects (A↔C).
