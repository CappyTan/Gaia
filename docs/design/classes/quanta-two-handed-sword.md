## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** Timeline Breaker · **Attunement × Archetype:** QUANTA × Two-Handed Sword
- **Primary stat:** SPD (← QUANTA) · **Secondary stat:** STR (← Two-Handed Sword) — a SPD/STR
  momentum line-cleaver *(from-brief)*
- **Resource:** QUANTA (party-shared; **fluctuates** — generates in bursts)
- **Attunement signature:** **probability swings** — crit / dodge / time, **no DoT**. QUANTA's
  "DoT-that-isn't" is **Doom** (a delayed, *determined* hit — nothing ticks). Avoidance is a single
  axis, **dodge %** (canon: no separate untargetable/immune state; "can't be hit" = dodge toward
  ~100%). The accuracy/crit debuff is **Decohere**. The signature beat is **Superposition →
  Collapse** — reused here **across the whole LINE**, not one foe.

**Fantasy.** *(from-brief)* The Timeline Breaker is the **momentum/time line-cleaver** — the Reaver
whose great arc has *already landed* before it moves. He winds the heavy blade back and **compresses
time** into the swing: the longer the wind-up, the more of the future he folds into it, until the
cleave's outcome is **already written** and the whole enemy line's fate **collapses at once**. Where
the Causality Arbiter (QUANTA Hammer) seals a single foe's verdict on a determined *point*, the
Timeline Breaker superposes the *entire line* and **collapses the arc** — a guaranteed massive sweep
across every foe at once. He is not the dagger that steals turns (The Anomaly) nor the duelist that
controls space (Phasewalker): he is **mass in motion through time**, the wide swing whose result is
foregone. He stamps **Doom** and **Decohere** down the reach of the blade so the line *fails* while
he winds, and if a cleave ever misses he **rewinds** the swing and takes it again. Slow to start,
inevitable to finish.

### The shared Reaver DNA *(from-brief — how this is a Two-Handed Sword)*

1. **Cleave** — the arc is melee **AoE**: it lands on the whole enemy line, the line-clearer (vs the
   Hammer's single crushing point). Here the cleave's outcome is a **collapsed certainty** — a
   determined sweep, not a hopeful one.
2. **Reach / zone-control** — the long blade zones space: **Doom** and **Decohere** are stamped
   *across the line* and into the back row, controlling the field by the arc's threat (outcome
   control at reach, not position-shoving).
3. **Momentum** — slow (`spd 7`), but the **wind-up compresses time**: force builds across the swing,
   and the release is cataclysmic. The "already landed" beat is the class's heartbeat.
4. **Opening → Finisher reuses QUANTA's own phase chain across the LINE.** No new combo resource: the
   Opening **is** accumulated **Superposition** spread over the whole enemy line (built by autos,
   wind-ups, and reach-stamps); the Finisher **Collapses** that superposition into a determined,
   guaranteed cleave across every foe.

**Build axes:** burst-now collapse ↔ momentum-reliability (A↔B) · momentum-self ↔ reach-attrition
(B↔C) · own-line damage ↔ enemy-line denial (A ↔ C) · certainty-on-my-cleave ↔ certainty-on-their-doom
(A ↔ C).

**Cross-lane synergy:** **C stamps Doom + Decohere across the line and superposes its fate → B
compresses the wind-up so the determined arc comes fast (and rewinds it if it strays) → A collapses
the superposition into one guaranteed massive cleave that lands on every Doomed, Decohered foe at
once.**

### Distinctness *(invariants #9 & #10 — how this seat is honored)*

- **Same-archetype (#9) — vs the four other Two-Handed Swords (the Reaver family).** Timeline Breaker
  is the **momentum/time line-cleaver**: the wind-up compresses time and the line's fate collapses
  at once. Distinct from the NOX **Worldender** (freeze the line → shatter the frozen arc, raw
  STR+STR power), the SOL **Starbreaker** (fast radiant sweeps that *spread* Burn down the line), the
  ANIMA **Apex Dominion** (contagion-arcs + self-evolution, non-healer), and the UMBRAXIS
  **Singularity Reaver** (gravity-pull the line into the arc, then reave). His arc is *determined* —
  outcome-control and time-compression, not status, fire, growth, or gravity.
- **Same-archetype — vs his Hammer cousin the Causality Arbiter (QUANTA Hammer, "the point").** They
  share only the QUANTA **signature** (probability swings, Doom, Superposition → Collapse). Causality
  Arbiter is the **single-target** executioner: a Doom *verdict* on one foe, the determined *point*,
  with tempo-control (stagger, rewind own action, time-skip a foe). Timeline Breaker is **line-scale
  + momentum**: he superposes and collapses the *whole line's* fate in one cleave, and his "momentum"
  is a **wind-up that compresses time** (a charge/release tempo on his own swing), not turn-order
  manipulation of foes. The point seals one fate; the arc collapses the line's.
- **Same-archetype — vs the QUANTA daggers/swords.** The Anomaly (daggers) is **turn-economy** —
  steal turns, act more than anything. Phasewalker (swords) is **space** — blink, afterimage, phase
  through the blow. Timeline Breaker touches neither: no turn-steal, no phase/blink. His lever is
  **momentum (wind-up = time compression) + AoE determined cleave** — the only QUANTA class whose
  signature is the *whole line collapsed at once*.
- **Same-attunement (#10) — QUANTA concept budget.** He reuses the QUANTA *signature* freely
  (probability swings, Doom, Decohere, Superposition → Collapse) as the framework intends, but takes
  the one role no other QUANTA class holds: **AoE / line-scale determined collapse + the time-compressed
  wind-up.** He does **not** pile onto a saturated QUANTA role — the **support/enchanter** seat is the
  Staff (Chronosage), the **evasion-tank** the Sword & Shield (Paradox Bastion), the **single-target
  crit/Doom executioner** the Hammer (Causality Arbiter), the **turn-economy assassin** the daggers
  (The Anomaly), and the **evasive phase-duelist** the swords (Phasewalker). The line-cleaver seat is
  held by no other QUANTA class. (QUANTA "no DoT" honored: Doom is a delayed *determined* hit, nothing
  ticks; avoidance is dodge %, no immune state.)
