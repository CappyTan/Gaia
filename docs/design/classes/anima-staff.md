# Genesis Sage — ANIMA × Staff

> **Status:** greenfield spec (Phase 2 kit). Lanes from the [Staff family note](./staff-family.md);
> every ability `proposed`. Numberless. Mechanics from the ratified
> [Attunement Mechanics Framework](../attunement-mechanics.md). Energy operation: **create**. The
> party's **dedicated main healer** (VIT+VIT — purest channel); distinct from the ANIMA S&S summoner
> and the ANIMA Hammer's *secondary* heal. Leans creation / living-light, not beasts or blood.

## Identity (derived + DNA)

- **Class:** Genesis Sage · **Attunement × Archetype:** ANIMA × Staff
- **Primary stat:** VIT (← ANIMA) · **Secondary stat:** VIT (← Staff) — the purest channel
- **Resource:** ANIMA (party-shared; **compounds**) · **Signature:** Regen / Bloom / creation
- **Energy op:** **Create** — make energy/matter/life from nothing. Caster role: **dedicated healer**.

**Fantasy.** The Genesis Sage is creation itself — a big bang in a staff. Where others manipulate what
exists, the Sage *makes* what doesn't: new life poured into the wounded, wards and healing motes
conjured from nothing, growth seeded where there was none. The party's wellspring and main healer —
the purest channel of life-as-energy.

### Lanes *(three flavors of healing & creation)*

| Lane | Identity | Best when |
|---|---|---|
| **A · Wellspring** | direct & reactive healing — big heals, emergency saves, revives | the main-healer seat |
| **B · Genesis** | creation — conjure barriers, shields, healing motes/wisps from nothing | vs burst; pre-shielding |
| **C · Flourish** | growth-buffs — Regen, Bloom, vitality & power that grows | long fights; buff-stacking comps |

**Build axes:** reactive heal ↔ created wards (A↔B) · healing ↔ growth-buffs (A,B↔C).
