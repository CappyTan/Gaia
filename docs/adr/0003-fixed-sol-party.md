# A fixed all-SOL party for the POC

In a game called *A World of Five Powers*, the POC party is **all one power (SOL)**: four
fixed characters, one per art-backed weapon type (Sword & Shield, Dual Swords, Staff,
Spellblade). A reader will reasonably ask "why only one of the five powers?"

**Why:** Dara has finished loot art for **only the SOL attunement** (four weapon charts).
An all-SOL party means every loot drop in the slice uses his real art, so the headline
Diablo-loot loop looks polished end to end. The Five Powers system is still exercised — but
through **enemy** attunements (the affinity ring applies when the SOL party hits bandits of
other powers), not through party diversity.

**Trade-off:** the player doesn't feel the five powers from their own roster in this slice.
We judged a polished, fully-art-backed single-power party better for a POC than a
mixed-power party with placeholder art for three-fifths of its drops.

**Reversible:** once art exists for other attunements (or we generate it), opening the
party to multiple powers — or a player-chosen roster from the 45 — is straightforward.
